Frequently asked question

When does the film start? ?

Since the sun sets at different times in our different locations, we can't predict when we'll start the movie exactly, but the gates open at 6pm (except in Auckland, where they open at 7pm), so come early for a sunset drink, a meal and live music (weekends only) and enjoy the fresh air! If you can't make it to 18:00, we recommend checking the time the sun sets in your area, as the film will start 10-15 minutes after the appropriate time.
